About PaycheckTools

PaycheckTools is a free online platform designed to help employees, freelancers, and independent contractors better understand their income. We provide simple and practical calculators for paycheck, salary, tax, commission, and related earnings scenarios.

Our goal is to make income calculations easier and more transparent. Many people receive their paychecks without fully understanding how taxes, deductions, or commissions affect their final take-home pay. PaycheckTools helps bridge that gap by offering easy-to-use tools with clear results.

Who We Serve

  • Employees who want to estimate their take-home pay
  • Freelancers and contractors calculating project income
  • Sales professionals estimating commissions
  • Anyone who wants a quick income calculation without complex spreadsheets
