About the Self-Employment Tax Calculator

This Self-Employment Tax Calculator helps freelancers, independent contractors, and small business owners quickly estimate their self-employment taxes based on net earnings. By entering your annual income and applicable tax rate, you can calculate the portion of your paycheck that will go toward Social Security and Medicare taxes. Understanding your self-employment tax is essential for budgeting, planning quarterly payments, and ensuring compliance with tax obligations.

Knowing your expected tax amount helps you manage income and avoid surprises at tax time. Simply input your net earnings and tax rate to instantly see your estimated tax liability and better plan your finances as a self-employed professional.
