Net Pay Calculator FAQs

What is net pay?

Net pay is the amount of income you take home after federal and state taxes are deducted from your gross income.

How does this net pay calculator work?

This calculator applies the entered federal and state tax rates to your income amount and estimates take-home pay based on the selected pay frequency.

Is the calculated net pay accurate?

The result is an estimate. Actual net pay may vary due to deductions, benefits, local taxes, and employer payroll policies.

Does this net pay calculator include deductions like insurance or retirement?

No. Only basic federal and state tax percentages are applied. Other deductions are not included.
