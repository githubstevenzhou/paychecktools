About the Paycheck Calculator

The Paycheck Calculator helps you quickly determine your gross pay, taxes, and net pay based on your hourly wage, total hours worked, and any overtime hours. By selecting your pay frequency and entering federal and state tax rates, you can accurately estimate your paycheck for weekly, biweekly, semimonthly, or monthly periods.

This calculator is ideal for employees and freelancers who want to track their income, manage paychecks, and plan budgets effectively. Use this tool to better understand how your salary translates into take-home pay after taxes.
