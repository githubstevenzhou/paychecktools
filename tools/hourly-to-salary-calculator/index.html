Hourly to Salary Calculator FAQs

How does an hourly to salary calculator work?

The calculator multiplies your hourly wage by your weekly working hours and the number of weeks you work per year to estimate your annual salary.

What hours per week should I use?

If you work full-time, 40 hours per week is common. For part-time or variable schedules, use your average weekly hours for a more accurate result.

Should I include paid vacation or holidays?

If your employer pays you during vacations or holidays, you can include those weeks. If time off is unpaid, subtract those weeks from your yearly total.

Does this calculator include taxes or deductions?

No. This tool calculates gross annual salary only. Taxes, insurance, retirement contributions, and other deductions are not included.
