How is overtime pay calculated?

When does overtime pay usually apply?

In the United States, overtime pay generally applies to hours worked over 40 hours per week for non-exempt employees. Specific overtime rules may vary by state law or employer policy.

Does this overtime pay calculator include taxes?

Yes. You can enter estimated federal and state tax rates, and the calculator will apply them to your gross pay to estimate your net take-home income. Actual payroll deductions may differ.

Can salaried employees earn overtime pay?

Some salaried employees may qualify for overtime depending on their job duties and classification. This calculator is primarily designed for hourly workers, as overtime rules for salaried employees can be more complex.
