Overtime Pay Calculator FAQs

How is overtime pay calculated?

Overtime pay is calculated by multiplying your hourly wage by the overtime rate multiplier and the number of overtime hours worked.

What is the standard overtime rate?

In many regions, the standard overtime rate is 1.5 times the regular hourly wage, though actual rules may vary by employer or location.

Does this overtime pay calculator include taxes?

Yes. This calculator applies the entered federal and state tax percentages to estimate your net pay after taxes.

Is overtime pay taxed differently than regular pay?

Overtime pay is generally taxed the same as regular income, although higher earnings may increase total tax withheld.
